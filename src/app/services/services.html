Tooth Whitening

This is a simple, affective and painless way of improving your smile. We take impressions to provide you with custom made trays. The trays are used at home with a whitening compound.

Porcelain Veneers

Porcelain Veneers are thin layers of porcelain which are cemented over the front of natural teeth. They have a very natural appearance and can be used to alter the shape and colour of your teeth.

Crowns and Bridges

Crowns can help improve the appearance of a discoloured or poorly shaped tooth. Crowns are often the best way of protecting heavily filled teeth from fracturing as they cover the tooth. The crown is carefully matched to the colour of the existing teeth.

A bridge replaces a missing tooth or teeth using the teeth on either side of the gap. In some instances an adhesive bridge can be provided which requires minimal tooth preparation.

Dentures

We can provide very natural looking dentures to replace missing teeth.

Tooth Coloured Fillings

Old amalgam fillings can be replaced with tooth coloured filling materials.

We can also offer composite reneers which require little or no tooth preparation, to help enhance aesthetics

Children

We aim to offer children using the practice a positive experience. We encourage regular visits and are happy to offer advice on your childs oral health.

Healthy Gums

The dental hygienists work closely together with the dentists to help care for your teeth and gums. It is important to treat gum disease to prevent tooth loss.

The early stages of gum disease are often easy to treat, by effective and thorough cleaning of your teeth. We can help you achieve this.

Oral Cancer Check

A routine dental examination plays an important part in the early detection of oral cancer. Mouth cancer is curable, small cancers are more easily treated.

Oral cancer represents 1% of all cancers in the UK and with around 4000 new cases reported each year in the UK the numbers are increasing.

The dentist will examine your whole mouth, checking your lips, cheeks, tongue, palate and throat to make sure that there are no changes in the tissue that which could indicate the onset of oral cancer.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a dental procedure used to treat infected nerve tissue within the tooth. It is carried out out under local anesthetic.
